Contribute

We welcome contributions in many forms. Our project has three open source components:

  • Front-end website (this page): pabulib/pabulib_front
  • Checker (validation tool): pabulib/checker
  • Process data repository: pabulib/pabulib_process_data

Ways you can help:

  • Share raw data (excel, csv)
  • Connect us to a city with Participatory Budgeting (PB) data — we can help collect it
  • Upload a ready .pb file via the Upload tab
  • Open a Pull Request / Issue in one of the repositories above.
  • Have an idea to add/improve something or found a bug? Send us an email.

Any help, suggestion, or just a comment is much appreciated — but please be kind :) as we sometimes don't have as much time as we'd like. Cheers!
